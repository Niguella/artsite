  • ART SIM RACING

    We are an 18+ group of like minded Sim racers, primarily playing Project CARS 2. We are mostly
    in and around Europe but we also have a few Americans in our ranks.

    Naturally we also play games like ETS 2, rFactor 2, Dirt Rally and can be found letting our
    eyebrows down and playing Golf It or the odd shooter.

    A usual week pans out as thus:

    Monday: Endurance Race
     A short qualify followed by a 1 hour race.

    Tuesday: Fun Night
     Tuesday is FUN NIGHT!! From cars that flat out want to kill you to Nordschliefe in the snow with RX. Always good fun!!

    Wednesday: League Night
     We usually run 2 leagues at any given time and alternate each Wednesdayfor the league, at the time of writing we are currently running a Group C and a GT3 league, both of which are team based.

    Practice & Qualifying is usually up around 7PM GMT with the race to follow.

    We run four dedicated servers on Project Cars 2.

    Join us on Discord or visit the Steam page for the latest updates.
